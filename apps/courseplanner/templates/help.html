Need Help?

Getting Started

First, go to the Create Course page under the Courses menu, and create a course. The courses you create will be displayed on the All Courses page, and also become searchable on the Course Search page.
On the Course Search page, when you click Submit on the Course Search form, you can then add a course to your planner, edit a course, or delete the course from your planner. Your planner on the home page will be updated with your changes accordingly.

Calculating Grades

Once you have added courses to your planner, you can calculate your grade for a course and your overall GPA after taking a course on the Grade Calculator page. First, choose a course from the dropdown menu, and click Add Row to enter an assignment grade. Once you are done adding your assignment grades, you can click Save.
Your overall grade in the class according to the assignment grades you have entered will appear in the Average Grade box. Click on the dropdown menu next to the box and select the letter that matches your grade. When you are finished, click Submit Grade to see the course grade added to the GPA Calculator. Your GPA and Credits will be updated according to your changes.

Sharing Planners

To help your academic planning, you can view a peer's planner! Head over to the View a Peer's Planner page and search by user or major, and click on a user to view their planner. You can also share your planner with other users on the Course Planner page by clicking the Share My Planner Publicly button. Note that you must have a university selected in your Profile in order to share your planner. If you would like to stop sharing your planner, you can click Unshare My Planner.
